Speckle's June webinar asks what it takes to make model data credible enough for AI workflows. Murat Melek from Suffolk Construction and Ryan Haunfelder from HGA share how their teams started extracting value from BIM portfolios, including what worked, what did not, and why broad lightweight extraction can be more useful than waiting for a perfectly conditioned subset.

The session covers a practical framework for validating and conditioning design data from historical and active projects, with a look at how Speckle can make that process continuous, automated, and actionable.
