BILT is an annual event, designed to cater to the needs of those who design, build, operate and maintain our built environment. As a community of professionals, it is committed to improving the many ways the industry works together. BILT EUROPE 2024 will be held 07 - 09 MAY, 2024, at RIGA, LATVIA.

This year, there will be number of open source talks being given, including 3 classes by Speckle, 1 double lab about the BlenderBIM Add-on, and 1 class from ThatOpenCompany.
