In this monthly meetup we focus on structural analysis and design with Richard Brice who will talk about BridgeLink, an open-source bridge engineer software published by the Washington State Department of Transportation. The flagship application, PGSuper for design, analysis, and load rating of precast, prestressed concrete girder bridges, will be highlighted including recent efforts to support bridge data exchanges with IFC 4x3.

The streaming will take place at <a href="https://meet.jit.si/openbim">https://meet.jit.si/openbim</a> @ 20:00 UTC, Saturday the 23rd of October

More info in this <a href="https://community.osarch.org/discussion/769/monthly-meetup-19-saturday-23rd-of-october-20-00-utc#latest">discussion</a>.

Thank you and see you then!
