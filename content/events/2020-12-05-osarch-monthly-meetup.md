For our 9th monthly meetup, Andrew Peel <a href="https://community.osarch.org/profile/Andyrexic" rel="nofollow">@Andyrexic</a> will give the keynote presentation on <a href="https://creativedesigner3d.com/2020/11/12/home-builder-asset-library/" rel="nofollow">Home Builder</a>, an open-source asset library for Blender that focuses on the process of creating interior architectural spaces with a wide variety of parametric assets and features to make designing interior spaces easy for beginners while also providing builders the engineering information they need to build the physical objects. Have a look also at this <a href="https://community.osarch.org/discussion/221/home-builder-for-blender/p1" rel="nofollow">thread</a> for comments and discussions of the community in the project.
