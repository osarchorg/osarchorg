[BIM World Munich 2026](https://www.bim-world.de/) takes place on 24–25 November at the ICM – International Congress Center Messe München. The tenth edition combines a two-day international congress, trade fair, open forums, and the BIM Town Innovation Area for startups and pitch sessions.

The event focuses on digitalisation across construction, real estate, and facility management, including BIM applications, digital twins, infrastructure, circular construction, sustainability, and building operations.

[Speckle lists BIM World Munich among its upcoming industry events](https://speckle.systems/speckle-events/) and will have team members there to discuss connected model data, collaboration, automation, and digital delivery across AEC projects.
