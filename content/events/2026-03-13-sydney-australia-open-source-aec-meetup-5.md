A heads up that there is another Sydney Australia open source AEC meetup on Friday March 13, 2026, from 9am to 1pm. It is a physical meetup at Level 14, ITS Tower Three, 300 Barangaroo Ave, with lunch afterwards for those who can stay.

Bring questions, a laptop, screenshots, demos, or anything else you are working on. The agenda includes presentations from OpenBIM Pledge signatories, bSDD, software guides, geometry, shape aspects, constituent correlations, and turning requirements into IDS.

More details are in the OSArch community thread: <https://community.osarch.org/discussion/comment/28324/#Comment_28324>
