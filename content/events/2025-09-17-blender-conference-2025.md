Blender Conference 2025 takes place from September 17-19 at Felix Meritis in Amsterdam. It is the main annual gathering for Blender artists, developers, contributors, and users, with four parallel tracks of talks, tutorials, meetings, and community sessions.

The 2025 schedule includes topics around geometry nodes, pipelines, USD, photogrammetry, scripting, robotics, and architectural visualisation.

Sessions are recorded and shared online. See the conference site and schedule for details: <https://conference.blender.org/2025/>
