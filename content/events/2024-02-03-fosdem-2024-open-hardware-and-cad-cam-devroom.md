FOSDEM is a free event for software developers to meet, share ideas and collaborate. Every year, thousands of developers of free and open source software from all over the world gather at the event in Brussels.

This year, the Open Hardware and CAD/CAM devroom includes talks by legend Thomas Krijnen on Multi-disciplinary geometry (libraries) in BIM and the IfcOpenShell software library, and legend Yorik van Havre on FreeCAD - state of the union.
