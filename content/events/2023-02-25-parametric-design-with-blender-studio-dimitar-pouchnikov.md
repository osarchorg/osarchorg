In a field overflowing with various different applications used for architectural concept design, Blender significantly stands out as a program that is; highly capable of extensive modelling tools including subdivision, widely accessible as it’s free and open source, inherently parametric, highly expandable as it has excellent addons like Tissue for tessellation and many others, and it’s equally excellent for presentation with Cycles rendering engine and Eevee.
