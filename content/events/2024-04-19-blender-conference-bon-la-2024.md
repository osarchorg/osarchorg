For the first time ever, there will be an official Blender Conference in the United States. Taking place on April 19 and 20 in Hollywood, Los Angeles. The event has already sold out, but the sessions will be recorded and shared on the CG Cookie YouTube channel later. See the schedule here, in particular the "3D in Construction" talk by Daniel Bridges, highlighting the BlenderBIM Add-on.
