<strong>About ArBIM</strong>

On the first Thursday of each month ArBIM, a Spanish-speaking community, meets online to learn and discuss about Open Source Architecture Software and Open BIM. Learn more by visiting <a href="http://arbim.org/">arbim.org</a> and the Spanish / Español section of the OSArch forum <a href="https://community.osarch.org/categories/espa%C3%B1ol-spanish">https://community.osarch.org/categories/español-spanish</a>

With members from Argentina, Spain, Paraguay and Chile, ArBIM's mission is to spread the use and support the development of free and open source BIM systems.
