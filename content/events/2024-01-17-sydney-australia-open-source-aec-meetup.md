A small casual social, show and tell, and Q&amp;A meetup will be held at Lendlease Australia offices all about open source in AEC.

Starting 10am Jan 17th Wednesday. Come to Level 14, ITS Tower Three, 300 Barangaroo Ave and there should be a room booked for us for the Sydney Australia open source AEC meetup. If you'd like, send me a message / email (dion@thinkmoult.com) and I'll let you know my phone number in case something goes wrong. We can start in the morning say around 10am and if people want to stay longer through lunch and beyond they can or if they want to drop off early they can as well :) We can play it by ear.

It'll be a casual combo of social, show and tell, and Q&amp;A.

There was an email thread where everyone suggested ideas on topics they were interested in:
<ul>
 	<li>IDS (ways to format output and what are current limitations) &amp; bsDD general ideology;</li>
 	<li>IFC schema deep dive into the structure and relationships;</li>
 	<li>IfcOpenshell code writing tips, anything around automating validation from multiple IFC files or is IDS just as flexible</li>
 	<li>Always keen for Blender and Blender BIM tips and tricks.</li>
 	<li>The IDS process and technology stack</li>
 	<li>Headless checking/dashboarding</li>
 	<li>BlenderBIM capabilities, in general</li>
 	<li>ifcOpenShell, geo-referencing, ifc model viewing…</li>
</ul>
If you haven't RSVPed please get in touch if coming and even better, bring something fun to showcase! Any FOSS is on-topic: Speckle, QGIS, CloudCompare, IFCJS, Godot...
