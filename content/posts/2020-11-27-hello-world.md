OSArch is a community of those who believe in creating a built environment with **free software** , increased **transparency** , and a more **ethical approach**. For 10 months, we've been helping build free software, writing tutorials and documentation, provide discussion forums, and host a wiki of everything you need to know about free software for the **architecture, engineering, and construction** industry.

Today, we're starting a new initiative to provide a outlet for you to stay in touch with all the **news and events** around free software in the AEC industry.

Let's change the industry together. Join OSArch today!
