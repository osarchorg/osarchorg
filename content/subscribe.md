Alternatively, you can follow OSArch news with a feed reader. A feed reader is an app or website that checks for new posts and shows them in one place, without needing email newsletters or social media.

Our news feed is:

[https://osarch.org/index.xml](/index.xml)

You can also subscribe only to posts about a specific topic:

{{< tag-feeds >}}

To subscribe:

1. Install or open a feed reader.
2. Add the OSArch feed URL above.
3. New OSArch posts will appear automatically when they are published.

Open source feed readers you can use include:

- [Thunderbird](https://www.thunderbird.net/)
- [FreshRSS](https://freshrss.org/)
- [NetNewsWire](https://netnewswire.com/)
- [Liferea](https://lzone.de/liferea/)
- [Akregator](https://apps.kde.org/akregator/)
- [Feeder](https://github.com/spacecowboy/Feeder)

If you already use a feed reader, you can usually copy the feed link and paste it into “Add feed”, “Subscribe”, or “Follow website”.
